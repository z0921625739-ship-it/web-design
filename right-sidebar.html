A Date with Death is a supernatural romance chat simulator visual novel about the week long bet you enter against the Grim Reaper to attempt to keep your soul. Create your own character, from appearance to pronouns, decorate your apartment, and spend the (possible) last week of your life getting to know the one hunting for your soul.

A Date with Death ZERO WEBcomic Out Now!

The world turns endlessly. People live, people die, and the Grim Reapers of the Underworld ensure the cycle remains unbroken. Nameless and unseen, these Reapers ferry souls to their final rest. In the 13th station, a new Reaper is "born." Known only as 8394, he awakens with no memories, no past—just a scythe and a duty. As he's thrust into the Underworld, he must confront what it truly means to carry the weight of death itself.

A Date with Death ZERO is our free prequel webcomic to A Date with Death that follow Casper in the years after his 'creation' as a Grim Reaper! If you've been wanting some more ADWD content after Beyond the Bet, this is a great stepping stone to future content ;) Read it on Webtoon or Tapas!
